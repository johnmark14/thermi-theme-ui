products

Water Bottle

Lightweight and compact Water Bottle that supports daily hydration. We have adopted a high-quality resin material that is suitable for everyday use and is not easily scratched or cracked. By adding water, the transparency of the material and the beauty of the form stand out more.

Workout Bottle

Work out bottle that supports an active and personal sports life. Since the spout can be removed, it is designed to be easy to put in ice and easy to wash. Since it has a scale, it is easy to make sports drinks, etc., and you can grasp the intake amount.

To Go Bottle

To Go Bottle for those who enjoy a calm time while taking a break. The double-structured bottle, which allows you to enjoy the feeling of floating, keeps the ice cold for a long time without melting. The mouthpiece is a soft silicone material and is a straw type with a pleasant mouthfeel.

Active Tumbler

Active Tumbler for a sports life where you can move your in the sun. The spout-type mouthpiece, which is easy to open and close, is comfortable to drink. It is easy to put ice and drinks, and the vacuum double structure keeps it cold for a long time. With an elastomer on the bottom, it softens the impact and noise when placed on the floor or ground of the studio.

Play Tumbler

Play Tumbler for kids who are crazy about playing under the blue sky. It is a straw type that is easy to drink even for small children, and it has a pleasant taste and you can also remove the straw inside and drink. The vacuum double structure makes it difficult for ice to melt and keeps it cold for a long time. With a handle, it is a design that is easy to hold even with a small hand.
